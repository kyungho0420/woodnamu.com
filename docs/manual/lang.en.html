Page by Sigonggan, User Guide

Page by Sigonggan is a Single Page website creation service provided by Web Freelancer Maxim.

Part 1: Introduction

1-1. What is Page by Sigonggan?

Page by Sigonggan is a Single Page website creation service for individuals and small businesses. Based on pre-designed templates and components, it helps you launch your business online quickly and at a reasonable cost.

1-2. Recommended For

A Single Page website is most effective for conveying a single, clear purpose and is especially recommended for the following people:

👍 Recommended For

  • Artists, writers, and developers who want to showcase their work to the world.
  • Business owners who want to promote their attractive spaces (cafes, restaurants, workshops, etc.).
  • Planners who want to introduce products or services with impact.
  • Anyone who wants to build their personal brand.
  • Those who need a special page for events and campaigns.

👎 Not Recommended For

  • Communities that require complex features like user registration and forums.
  • Information portals that need to provide a large amount of structured information.
  • Cases where real-time reservation or payment systems are essential.
If you require such features, please contact Maxim separately.

Part 2: Service Details

2-1. Single Page Concept

A Single Page website is a modern format that presents all information sequentially on one long page, eliminating the need to navigate through multiple pages. Visitors can easily and quickly browse all content just by scrolling.

  • Advantages: It offers an intuitive user experience, is optimized for mobile environments, and can deliver a focused message effectively.
  • Disadvantages: It is unsuitable for very large amounts of content and may be less favorable for search engine exposure across various keywords.

2-2. Content Preparation & AI Usage

Page by Sigonggan offers two ways to prepare content based on your level of involvement.

  • AI Plan: Simply provide essential information like your website's purpose and core services. Our AI will actively engage in the creation process to complete the detailed content (text, images, etc.).
  • Custom Plan: You submit all text and images for the site through our application form. If needed, you can also request AI content generation support for specific parts.
To avoid affecting site loading speed, images and videos are optimized and compressed. Videos longer than 10 seconds are uploaded to YouTube and then linked.

2-3. Domain Connection

Page by Sigonggan provides a default domain in the format of ID.sigonggan.com for free. If you have your own personal domain, you can connect it. If you do not have a domain but need one, you can request our domain registration service.

2-4. Hosting & Security

All sites come with stable hosting that includes Cloudflare security and an SSL certificate by default. While there are no technical limits on storage and traffic, a separate consultation may be required for excessive traffic that falls outside the scope of a typical website to ensure stable service.

2-5. Email Service

If you connect a personal domain, we offer a free email routing service that allows you to create an email address with your domain (e.g., `contact@mydomain.com`) and have messages forwarded to your existing personal email account (like Gmail, Naver, etc.).

Part 3: Application

3-1. Pricing

The Page by Sigonggan service consists of a one-time setup fee and an annual fee that is renewed each year. The annual fee includes hosting, security certificate, and basic maintenance costs.

Check prices on the official website

3-2. Production Process

The website production process consists of the following four steps:

  1. Consultation & Payment: We identify the purpose and requirements of your website, conduct a service consultation, and then complete the payment.
  2. Content Preparation: You prepare and submit the necessary materials for your website according to the selected plan (AI or Custom).
  3. Production & Feedback: We create the website based on the submitted materials and share intermediate results to incorporate your feedback.
  4. Final Completion & Launch: Once the final review is complete, the website is officially launched, and maintenance management begins.

3-3. Payment Method

Payment is only possible via bank transfer, and the following currencies are accepted:

  • Korean Won (KRW)
  • US Dollar (USD)
  • Vietnamese Dong (VND)

3-4. License

The ownership of the source code and design of websites created through Page by Sigonggan belongs to 'Maxim'. The client has the 'right to use' the website during the contract period. When the contract ends, the right to use also expires.

Part 4: Maintenance

4-1. SEO

Once production is complete, we will register your website with major search engines like Google and Naver for free. Page by Sigonggan is also built in compliance with basic Search Engine Optimization (SEO) rules.

Ranking high in search results is determined by various external factors such as content quality and competition, and therefore cannot be guaranteed.

4-2. Content Modification

If content changes are needed during operation, revisions are available within the annual free modification limit. Exceeding this limit will incur a fee.

  • Free Modification: Changes to text and images within the annual provided count.
  • Paid Modification: Exceeding the basic count, changing the design (layout), adding features, etc.

4-3. Technical Support

The scope of technical support for stable operation is as follows:

  • Free Support: Problems within our production scope, such as website access failure or functional errors.
  • No Support (or Paid): Support not directly related to the Page by Sigonggan service, such as solving a client's PC problems or guiding on how to use third-party programs.

Part 5: Contract & Refund

5-1. Refund Policy

In case of early termination, only the 'annual fee' is refundable, excluding the 'setup fee'.

  1. The refundable amount is calculated based on the remaining months of the service period.
  2. A penalty and administrative fee of 20% is deducted from the calculated refundable amount.

5-2. Contract Expiration

After the contract expires, it will be processed as follows:

  • Waiting Status: The contract will be in a waiting state for 7 days after the expiration date, awaiting renewal.
  • Automatic Termination: If the contract is not renewed within the waiting period, it will be automatically terminated, and all data (text, photos, etc.) stored on the server will be permanently deleted.
